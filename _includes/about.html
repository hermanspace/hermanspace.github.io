About Me

I build web applications and internal systems for real-world workflows, mostly where teams need things to be fast, clear, and reliable.

My main playground is backend and fullstack development using PHP and Golang, with Laravel, Blade, Vue, and Progressive Web Apps (PWA) in the mix. I also build custom tools tailored to specific operational needs.

I am particularly interested in system architecture, networking, DevOps, and automation. In short: I like making software that works, scales, and does not panic on Monday morning.
